In Between

As you can possibly see, Mayajs is the combination of both Express and Nestjs. Like Express we utilize middlewares the same way which means all of the middlewares created using express can be used inside Mayajs. For Nestjs we also utilizes declarations, dependency injections and reflections. With this you can have the benefits of both framework in one package.
